PRIDE AND PREJUDICE

By Jane Austen

*Pride and Prejudice* is more than a love story; it’s a family comedy, a satire on Regency society, and a moral tale about not judging others too hastily. The quick-witted Elizabeth Bennet and the brooding yet kind-hearted Mr. Darcy make for one of literature’s most iconic couples.
