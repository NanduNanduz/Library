WUTHERING HEIGHTS

By Emily Brontë

*Wuthering Heights* is a haunting tale of passion, revenge, and tragedy set on the windswept Yorkshire moors. The novel centers on the intense, tumultuous relationship between Heathcliff and Catherine Earnshaw, whose love is both destructive and transcendent. With its dark atmosphere and unforgettable characters, Emily Brontë’s only novel is considered a cornerstone of English literature and one of the greatest love stories ever told.

Genres:

Classic, Gothic, Romance
