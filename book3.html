HARRY POTTER AND THE SORCERER'S STONE

By J.K. Rowling

*Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone* introduces us to the magical world of Hogwarts, where Harry, an orphaned boy, discovers his true identity as a wizard. With his newfound friends, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry embarks on a journey filled with spells, secrets, and the ultimate battle against dark forces led by Lord Voldemort. This enchanting tale of friendship, bravery, and magic is the first installment of the globally beloved Harry Potter series.

Genres:

Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult
