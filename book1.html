To Kill a Mockingbird

By Harper Lee

Published in 1960, *To Kill a Mockingbird* is a classic of modern American literature. The novel explores serious issues such as racial injustice and moral growth through the eyes of a young girl, Scout Finch. Set in the Deep South during the Great Depression, the book's compelling storytelling and memorable characters have earned it a permanent place in literary history.

Genres:

Fiction, Classic, Historical
